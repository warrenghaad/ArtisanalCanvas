# Axis 1: General Progression

1. Notice — What am I actually seeing?
2. Mark — What kind of line or pressure belongs here?
3. Measure — What is bigger, smaller, closer, farther?
4. Shape — What is the flat design?
5. Plane — What surface is facing me?
6. Volume — What solid form is underneath?
7. Rotate — How is it turning in space?
8. Attach — How do parts connect?
9. Transform — How can this form bend, stretch, break, or become something else?
10. Compose — How does the whole page hold together?
11. Express — What emotion or force is carried by the marks?
12. Integrate — Can I draw from life, memory, invention, and feeling together?
